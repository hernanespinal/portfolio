Awards

Resident of the Year

The Resident of the Year Award is presented by the Department of Residential Life at Loyola University New Orleans. This award is voted on by the community directors of the four residence halls to choose one resident who exemplifies the values set forth by the Department of Residential Life.

Delta Sigma Pi Most Valuable Brother

This award was awarded at the annual Rose Award Ceremony held each year by the Delta Nu Chapter of Delta Sigma Pi at Loyola University New Orleans. This is presented to the brother who exemplifies the ideals of the chapter of Professionalism, Service, and Leadership.

Champions of Character Team

Recognized as the male representative for Loyola Musco Lightning Champions of Character team.The institutional winners and those eligible for these top SSAC awards are selected based on the following criteria:
  • Quality GPA (determined by institution but encouraged to meet or exceed a 3.5)
  • Community Service
  • On the Field Excellence (must be a contributor to the success of the team)
  • Tenure at Institution (must have competed and been in residence at that institution for two years)
  • Must have displayed the virtues of the Champions of Character of integrity, respect, responsibility, sportsmanship, and servant leadership
